Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how FunWords ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our crossword puzzle game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe gaming experience.

Quick Summary: We don't collect any personal information. FunWords uses only Google Play Games Services for saving your progress and Google AdMob for ads. All data is handled by Google's secure systems.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect or store any personal information. FunWords uses only the following Google services:

Google Play Games Services

  • Game Progress: Your game saves, achievements, and leaderboard data are stored through Google Play Games Services
  • Player ID: An anonymous player identifier provided by Google Play Games
  • All data is managed by Google according to their privacy policy

Google AdMob

  • Advertising Data: AdMob may collect information to show relevant ads
  • Device Information: Limited technical information for ad delivery
  • All advertising data is handled by Google AdMob according to their privacy policy

How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect personal information directly, the services we use handle data as follows:

  • Google Play Games Services: Saves your game progress, achievements, and allows you to compete on leaderboards
  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements to support the free game experience

We have no access to personal information - all data handling is managed by Google's secure infrastructure.

Information Sharing

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The game uses only Google's services:

Google Play Games Services

Your game data is stored and managed entirely by Google Play Games Services. We have no access to this information. Google handles this data according to their Privacy Policy.

Google AdMob

Advertisements are served by Google AdMob. Any advertising-related data collection is handled by Google according to their Privacy Policy and AdMob Privacy Policy.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store personal data directly, your information security is handled by Google's industry-leading security infrastructure:

  • Google Play Games Services uses Google's secure data centers and encryption
  • Google AdMob follows Google's strict security and privacy standards
  • We have no servers or databases that store your personal information

Your Rights and Choices

Game Data Management

  • Google Play Games: You can manage your game data through your Google Play Games settings
  • Delete Data: You can delete your game progress through Google Play Games settings
  • Sign Out: You can disconnect from Google Play Games at any time

Advertising Preferences

  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's advertising settings
  • Visit Google's Ads Settings to manage ad preferences
  • Reset your advertising ID through your device settings

Children's Privacy

FunWords is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data according to applicable privacy laws.

Data Retention

Since we don't store any data directly:

  • Google Play Games Services manages retention of your game progress according to Google's policies
  • AdMob data is retained according to Google's advertising policies
  • We have no data to retain or delete on our end

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the game or by email. Your continued use of FunWords after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
